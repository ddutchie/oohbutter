## How to hand off assets

- Place all files in `/public/images/`
- Use the exact filenames listed below (or tell me your filenames and I'll update the code)
- Minimum resolution is listed per image — higher is fine, the browser will scale down
- JPG is preferred for photos; PNG if you need a transparent background

## Notes on photography style

Based on the brand guide, aim for:
- **Natural light** where possible
- **Warm tones** — the brand palette is butter yellows, earth greens, soft blues
- **Honest and unfussy** — food that looks real and delicious, not overly styled
- **People** — shots of Imke and Megan cooking, plating or smiling land better than pure food-only shots
